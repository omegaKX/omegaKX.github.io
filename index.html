Definition¶

The total number of each type of atom is conserved, and thus the mass does not change.

Proving the Law of Conservation of Mass with the equation $H_2O + Na = NaOH + H$¶

In [8]:
# H2O = 2 Hydrogen + 1 Oxygen
water = hydrogenMass*2 + oxygenMass
In [9]:
# Na = 1 Sodium
sodium = sodiumMass
In [10]:
# NaOH = 1 Sodium + 1 Hydrogen + 1 Oxygen
sodiumHydroxide = sodiumMass + hydrogenMass + oxygenMass
In [11]:
# H = Hydrogen
hydrogen = hydrogenMass
In [12]:
if water + sodium == sodiumHydroxide + hydrogen: print("The Law of Conservation of Mass is true")
else: print("The Law of Conservation of Mass is false")

The Law of Conservation of Mass is true

Thus the Law of Conservation of mass is true.
